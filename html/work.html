If you dive deep into the imagination of H. C. Andersen, you will find that he had a rather though life even though he was one of the most famous people of his time. In this concept we take all symbols of hope and warmth in the authors fairy tales and turn them into a flame that guides the user - not only through his fairy, but also the life of H.C Andersen himself.
